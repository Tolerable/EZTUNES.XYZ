Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

EZTunes.XYZ does not collect or store personal information. All usage data, including your music preferences and interactions, are stored locally on your device using your browser's storage. There is no backend server — the site is hosted via GitHub Pages.

Analytics and Tracking

We use Google Analytics to better understand how users interact with EZTunes.XYZ. This includes anonymous data such as page views, session duration, and city-level location data. We’ve configured it to work without cookies to maintain your privacy. No personal or identifying data is collected.

Use of Local Storage

Your activity, preferences, and any AI-generated track history or bookmarks are saved using your browser’s local storage only. This ensures a smooth user experience without needing an account or login.

Audio & AI Content

All tracks are AI-assisted creations and are served directly from the site or linked sources. When you use features like generating playlists or interacting with the AI music assistant, requests may be processed through third-party AI tools like Pollinations.ai. EZTunes.XYZ does not control how these tools cache data — please avoid sharing sensitive information through any input fields.

Cookies

No tracking cookies are used. All stored data is strictly local to your browser.

Contact

For any questions or privacy concerns, feel free to reach out at support@eztunes.xyz.
